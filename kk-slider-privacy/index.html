K.K. Slider Bot Privacy

2022-04-12 :: 2 min read (234 words)

oh hello there - you checked the privacy policy. good on you!

so, this one is going to be a bit shorter than other privacy policys you’ve seen. instead of being all like “ooooh we’re gonna take this data maybe if it’s the 9th of may and the moon is a little shiny”, i’m going to do it like this

  • going to set out what i collect
  • why i do it

but, if that’s too long, here’s the short version: i don’t track you, or sell any data. we don’t have the message content intent enabled on Discord’s side, so we can only read / commands directly tied to this bot.

SO - here we go!

Guild (Server) Identifiers⌗

This is a long numerical ID tied to your server, and is something like 123456789012345678. We use this to make sure your K.K’s preferences. We don’t share it.

Preferences⌗

Your preferences (at time of writing) include things like the voice channel we last connected to, your timezone, and the upcoming request query.

The future⌗

Future features might look at the permissions you have in Discord, to make sure you’re allowed to change the bots settings in that server. We won’t store any of this.

Future features also might add more data to our preference storing. Again, this won’t be used to track you, and we won’t share it.

TL;DR - We don’t track you! ~
